Who are we ?
Lotus Thanka is an online platform of the store where it has been trying to promote the local and culture of Nepal in the form of art and crafts. We have been trying to bring the Nepalese craft on hype all over the world. We are supplier, exporter and even manufacturer of Nepalese handicrafts and various Nepali products. We offer various Nepali products such as Tibetan items like singing bowls, pray wheels Tibetan book similarly thanka , pray beads as rudraksha and bodhi etc. Lotus Thanka Art and Handicraft is basically a store which provides souvenir products and gives its client a remembrance of Nepal. We are basically situated in Kathmandu Nepal and operate throughout the world.
Our Mission
To establish a goodwill and strong public relation with its customers as it stands with providing qualitative products and satisfy its valued customers.
Our Vision
To shop locally online as we believe internet is the best way to make easier purchases which will save up customers time and provide them their goods at competitive price.
